    About us

    Hotel Machhapuchhre Restaurant and Lodge is a charming establishment nestled in the heart of Jeetpur, Kapilvastu, Nepal. Situated in a serene and picturesque location, this lodge offers a tranquil retreat for travelers seeking comfort, convenience, and a taste of Nepali hospitality.

    The lodge boasts a traditional Nepali architectural style blended seamlessly with modern amenities. As guests step into the premises, they are greeted by the warm and welcoming ambiance that reflects the essence of Nepali culture and hospitality.

    Accommodation options at Hotel Machhapuchhre are designed to cater to the diverse needs of travelers. The lodge offers a range of well-appointed rooms, including deluxe rooms and suites, each exuding comfort and coziness. Whether guests are traveling solo, as a couple, or with family, there is a suitable accommodation option to ensure a pleasant stay.

    One of the highlights of Hotel Machhapuchhre is its restaurant, which serves an array of delectable Nepali, Indian, and international cuisines. Guests can indulge in flavorful dishes prepared by experienced chefs using fresh, locally sourced ingredients. From traditional Nepali dal-bhat-tarkari to continental delicacies, there is something to satisfy every palate.

    In addition to comfortable accommodation and mouthwatering cuisine, Hotel Machhapuchhre offers a range of facilities and services to enhance the guest experience. These include:

    • 24-hour front desk assistance: The friendly and attentive staff members are always available to cater to guests' needs, whether it's arranging transportation, providing local information, or addressing any concerns.
    • Wi-Fi connectivity: Guests can stay connected with friends, family, and work with complimentary Wi-Fi access available throughout the premises.
    • Room service: For those who prefer to dine in the comfort of their rooms, the lodge offers prompt and efficient room service, ensuring a hassle-free dining experience.
    • Conference and banquet facilities: Hotel Machhapuchhre is equipped with spacious venues ideal for hosting conferences, meetings, weddings, and other events. With state-of-the-art amenities and personalized service, the lodge ensures that every event is a success.
    • Sightseeing arrangements: The lodge can assist guests in arranging sightseeing tours to explore the nearby attractions, including historical sites, religious monuments, and natural wonders.

    Overall, Hotel Machhapuchhre Restaurant and Lodge offers a delightful blend of comfort, convenience, and Nepali hospitality, making it the perfect choice for travelers visiting Jeetpur, Kapilvastu, Nepal. Whether for leisure or business, guests are sure to have a memorable stay at this charming establishment.
